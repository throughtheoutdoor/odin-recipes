Vegemite on Toast

Description

Delicious crispy toast with a light spread of butter and an equal amount of vegemite. Also great with some cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Vegemite

Directions

  1. Toast the bread to your desired level of crispiness
  2. Spread butter on the hot toast
  3. Thinly spread a layer of Vegemite on the toast
  4. Optionally add a slice of your favourite cheese
