Lasagna

Description

Large flat noodles with alternating layers of delicious tomato sauce and cheese and basil. Baked in a hot oven until the cheese has melted and the flavours have combined.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Cheese
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Boil noodles until al dente, set aside
  2. Lightly spray an oven safe dish with cooking spray
  3. Cover the bottom of the dish with the noodles
  4. Spoon some tomato sauce onto the noodles to create a thin layer
  5. Sprinkle a little basil, cheese and oregano onto the sauce
  6. Repeat layering in this manner until alomst the top of the pan
  7. Top the stack of noodles with some more cheese
  8. Bake at 375 degrees until the cheese is bubbling and brown
