Tea

Description

a delicious hot beverage created by steeping dried tea leaves in hot water. THe addition of a small amount of cream and sugar are found by some to enhance the taste.

Ingredients

  • Tea, loose or bagged
  • Water
  • Optional milk, cream and sugar
  • Optional tea ball/bag if using loose tea

Directions

  1. Fill kettle with cold water, bring to almost boiling
  2. Place loose tea in an infuser, for loose tea
  3. Place a tea bag or infuser in a mug
  4. Fill mug with water
  5. Steep 3-5 minutes
  6. Add optional milk, cream and sugar
